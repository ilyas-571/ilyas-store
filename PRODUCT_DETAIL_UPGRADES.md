# Product Detail Page - Design Upgrades ✨

## Overview
The product detail page has been completely revamped with modern, eye-catching enhancements to improve visual hierarchy, user engagement, and overall experience.

---

## 🎨 Major Design Improvements

### 1. **Enhanced Image Gallery**
- **Larger thumbnails** with better visual feedback
- **Gradient backgrounds** (from slate-50 to slate-100) for premium feel
- **Smooth animations** and scaling effects on hover
- **Active state indicator** with ring offset and scale transforms
- **Image counter** badge (e.g., "1/5") in bottom right
- **Share button** directly on image with smooth styling
- **Shine effect** on hover for interactive feedback
- **Better zoom interactions** with improved cursor feedback

### 2. **Eye-Catching Product Badges**
- **Exclusif badge** with gradient (amber-500 to amber-600) and sparkle icon
- **Sale badge** with animated pulse effect and discount percentage
- **Rounded corners** and shadow effects instead of flat design
- **Better visual hierarchy** with icons and improved spacing

### 3. **Premium Price Display**
- **Gradient background box** (amber-50 to orange-50) for pricing section
- **Larger, bolder typography** for prices
- **Savings highlight** in red badge showing exact savings amount
- **Unit price** information when variants are selected
- **Border styling** with amber accent color

### 4. **Enhanced Rating & Reviews**
- **Visual rating card** with blue background and border
- **Larger star displays** with better visibility
- **Clickable rating** with chevron down indicator
- **Scroll-to-reviews** functionality on click
- **Improved typography** with better contrast

### 5. **Stock Status Redesign**
- **Color-coded backgrounds** (green for in-stock, red for out-of-stock)
- **Animated pulse effect** for low stock warnings
- **Emoji indicators** (⏰ for low stock, ✓ for available, 📭 for out of stock)
- **Better visual prominence** with padding and rounded corners
- **Bold typography** for better readability

### 6. **Improved Variant Selection**
- **Rounded buttons** with enhanced styling
- **Visual feedback** on selected variant (ring offset, scale)
- **Check mark indicator** showing selected option
- **Smooth hover effects** and transitions
- **Better disabled state** with opacity changes

### 7. **Call-To-Action Buttons**
- **Rounded corners** (rounded-lg) instead of sharp edges
- **Gradient backgrounds** for "Buy It Now" button
- **Icon additions** (ShoppingBag for Add to Cart, Zap for Buy Now)
- **Larger sizes** (h-16) for better mobile usability
- **Shadow effects** with hover shadow enhancement
- **Scale transform** on hover (hover:scale-105)
- **Better accessibility** with proper ARIA labels

### 8. **Trust Badges Enhancement**
- **Gradient background** from slate-50 to blue-50
- **Rounded corners** with box-shadow
- **Icon scale animations** on hover
- **Better color contrast** and visibility
- **Hover states** with background color changes
- **Cursor-help** for interactive feel

### 9. **Loading States**
- **Animated skeleton loaders** with gradient backgrounds
- **Better visual matching** with final design
- **Smooth animations** using Tailwind's animate-pulse
- **Proper spacing** and layout matching

### 10. **Information Tabs Section**
- **Larger, bolder typography** for tab labels
- **Gradient underline** indicator (amber-500 to orange-500)
- **Better spacing** and visual separation
- **Rounded corners** on content containers
